FAQ » Assessment

What do the assessment points mean?

This depends on your assessment policy. Learning Ladders is designed to fit around the assessment policy for your school and what each assessment point means is a decision which needs to be made in school.

When should I award each assessment point?

This depends on your assessment policy. Learning Ladders is designed to fit around the assessment policy for your school and when an assessment point should be awarded is a decision which needs to be made in school.

Why do I need to set my pupil starting points as 'Start of Year'?

'Start of Year' simply means the pupils came into the year with this knowledge already. All historical assessment information should be noted as 'Start of Year'.

Does Age Related Expectation within Learning Ladders include Reception?

Yes, you will need to backfill for Reception. When creating your own content you will also need to create a set of rung statements for Reception.

Can I import my assessment data from another tracking system into Learning Ladders?

No. Due to the fact that Learning Ladders uses your school's personalised curriculum and not broad performance descriptors, we are unable to map your assessment data from other tracking systems into Learning Ladders.
